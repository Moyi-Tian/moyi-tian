Moyi Tian

 

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado Boulder, working with Professor Nancy Rodriguez and Professor David Bortz.

I received my Ph.D in Applied Mathematics from Brown University in 2024, advised by Professor Björn Sandstede.

My research interests include nonlinear dynamical systems, pattern formation, network and data science, and inverse problems of learning dynamical systems models from data.
